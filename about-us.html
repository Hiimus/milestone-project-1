Frode, Gym Owner.

Frode is the owner of Styrk Gym. He has been in the game longer than most, and is still going strong. He has competed in the sport of powerlifting the last 20 years, and is not thinking about quitting just jet. "I have personally experienced the physical and mental benefits of strength training. It is therefore my goal to introduce as many people as I can to lifting. I love to share my experience so I can help, assist and get new friends along the way."

Arne, PT.

Arne is a certified personal trainer and weightlifter. He has helped many beginners start their lifting journey with his effective ways of teaching barbell movements. Arne believes good technique is a crucial part of longevity in the sport. "When I teach the lifts, I let them just try on their own with light weight, and then I adjust with small cues to make the lifts safe. From there they can learn and build confidence."

Pia, PT.

Pia is a certified personal trainer and a dedicated yogi. She is always in a good mood, and her positive energy is contagious to her surroundings. She motivates people in to excel from their current situation no matter where you are starting from. "The most important parts of the workout is showing up, and having fun. The rest will sort itself out."
